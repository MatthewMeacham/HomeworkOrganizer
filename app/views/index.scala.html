What is Orgnizer?

  • Orgnizer is a web application that helps students achieve more in their studies by providing a unique and innovative homework organizer.

What are the benefits of using Orgnizer?

  • Orgnizer enables students to set dates for assignments. Then, Orgnizer will organize and prioritize your assignments.
  • Orgnizer is online and therefore accessible nearly everywhere, which means no more forgetting of paper planners!
  • Orgnizer warns students of upcoming and late assignments.
  • Orgnizer will also archive finished assignments for reference.
  • Orgnizer enables parents to create accounts and monitor their children's assignments and classes.
  • Orgnizer furthers the educational benefits by enabling teachers to create accounts and create classes which students can easily join and receive assignments.
